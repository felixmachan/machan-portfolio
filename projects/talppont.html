Massage parlour website - Talppont

A static website for Talppont, a massage parlour in Vác, with the focus on calm and attracting design.

Web Design Static Site UI/UX

Problem

One of my close friends' mother wanted a simple and calming website for her massage parlour. She knew I was in web development and asked for my help. This is the result.

Goal

To design and develop a website, which makes it easy to get the most important information about the massage parlour and its services. For example the types of services and prices.

Approach

The main colors of the brand are royal blue and baby blue, so I had to make design around these colors. I wanted to create a calming atmosphere with the help of colors, fonts and images. I used a lot of white space to make the content more digestible.
