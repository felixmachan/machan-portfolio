Home IoT Management System - MPTRhome

Mechatronics build

A CNC-machined pedal set, combining hardware, sensing, and embedded control for sim racing.

Overview

MPTRhome is a modular home IoT management system designed to unify heterogeneous smart devices into a single, controllable platform. The system focuses on practical usability, local-first operation, and extensibility, targeting both commercial off-the-shelf and custom-built IoT hardware.

Problem

Most home automation ecosystems are fragmented: different protocols, closed ecosystems, cloud dependency, and limited customization. Integrating Zigbee, Wi-Fi, and custom IoT devices into a single system often requires multiple tools and complex configuration, which is inefficient and hard to maintain.

Goal

The goal was to design a centralized IoT management system that can discover, register, monitor, and control diverse devices through a unified interface, while remaining scalable, protocol-agnostic, and suitable for custom hardware integrations.

Approach

The system was designed around a modular backend architecture with clear separation between device discovery, communication, and data persistence. Standard protocols (e.g. Zigbee, MQTT, HTTP) are abstracted behind a common device model, allowing both commercial and custom IoT devices to be handled uniformly. Emphasis was placed on local operation, reliability, and long-term maintainability.
