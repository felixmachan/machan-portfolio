About

My name is Felix Machan and I'm a 23-year-old engineering student coming from a small town near Budapest, Hungary. Ever since I was a child, I have known that I'm mainly interested in technical subjects and I like building things. This is exactly why I chose to pursue engineering as my field of study. However, it was not clear to me at first which specific branch of engineering I wanted to specialize in. After some research, I found out about Mechatronics Engineering, which perfectly combines my interests in mechanical, electrical, and software engineering. With this multidisciplinary approach, I can work on a wide range of projects and solve complex problems that require knowledge from multiple fields.

Since the beginning of my studies, I have adopted a hands-on approach to system design and implementation. I build practical, end-to-end solutions spanning embedded systems, software, and infrastructure, with a strong emphasis on understanding how things work - not just making them work.

System Design

I approach problems by breaking them down into clear components and interfaces. hether it's an embedded system, a backend service, or a full project architecture, I focus on simplicity, scalability, and maintainability.

Reliability

I care about systems behaving predictably. That means proper error handling, monitoring, documentation, and designing with real-world constraints in mind - not just ideal conditions.

Delivery

I like turning ideas into working solutions. From prototyping and iteration to testing and refinement, I focus on delivering tangible results and continuously improving them based on feedback.

My attitude

I think the best engineers are lifelong learners who stay curious and adaptable. This is why I constantly seek out new knowledge and skills to expand my toolkit. Since my interests are broad, I enjoy exploring different fields and technologies, which helps me connect ideas from various domains to create innovative solutions. I embrace challenges as opportunities to grow, and I enjoy collaborating with others to share knowledge and build better solutions together.
